# Sprint Review Actas (Meeting Minutes)

This folder holds meeting minutes (actas) from sprint reviews and team synchronization sessions.

## File Naming Convention

Each sprint produces one acta file:

- `S0-acta.md` — Sprint 0 review acta
- `S1-acta.md` — Sprint 1 review acta
- `S2-acta.md` — Sprint 2 review acta
- etc.

## Contents

Each acta typically includes:

- Sprint number, dates, and attendees
- Summary of completed work and blockers
- Sprint retrospective (what went well, what to improve)
- Decisions made or confirmed during the sprint review
- Artifacts produced (links to PRs, releases, or updates)
- Outstanding action items and next-sprint preview

## Usage

Actas are referenced by the thesis (Chapter VI) as supporting evidence of the Scrum process and iterative development. They feed both the memoria workflow and the defense evidence pack.
